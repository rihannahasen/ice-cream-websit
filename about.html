About Gojo Ice Cream Gojo Ice Cream is a family-owned company that was founded in 2015. We are passionate about making ice cream that is not only delicious but also made with the finest ingredients. We use fresh, local ingredients whenever possible and we never use artificial flavors or preservatives. We believe that ice cream should be a treat that everyone can enjoy. That's why we offer a wide variety of flavors, including classic favorites like chocolate and vanilla, as well as more unique flavors like lavender and honey. We also offer a variety of dairy-free and vegan options so that everyone can find something they love. We are committed to using sustainable practices in our business. We source our ingredients from local farms whenever possible and we use recycled and recyclable materials in our packaging. We are also working to reduce our carbon footprint by using energy-efficient equipment and practices. We are proud to be a part of the community and we are always looking for ways to give back. We donate a portion of our profits to local charities and we support a number of community events. We hope you will try Gojo Ice Cream and experience the difference that our passion for quality and our commitment to sustainability can make.
